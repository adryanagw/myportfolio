About

who / what / how

I work at the point where raw data becomes a recommendation. That means writing SQL to interrogate a database, running regression and classification models to explain and predict behavior, and segmenting customers so teams know exactly who to talk to and why.

My projects below come from real-world style cases — e-commerce PayLater adoption, marketplace spending, grocery sales, banking segmentation, campaign evaluation, and a healthcare prediction problem. Each one ends with something actionable, not just a chart.

I care about clarity: a model is only useful if a non-technical person can understand what it's telling them — and what to do about it.
